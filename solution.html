Solutions

The industry needs to adopt a new approach by focusing on security operations. That’s where Traceback can help.


SWIFT DETECTION AND RESPONSE

Detect. Respond. Recover. Any threat, any time.

Detection and Response is key in responding to advanced threats. Enter Tracback's® Mobile Operations Centre. We eliminate alert fatigue and false positives to deliver a swift response with our in-house cloud security platform. Your Traceback Serviced Security® Team (SST) works directly with you to perform threat hunting, incident response, and guided remediation. Contact us today to find out how we can tailor our solutions to the specific needs of your organization.


PRE-EMPTIVE RISK MANAGEMENT

Inspect. Prevent. Eliminate. Any threat, any time.

Ensuring that your network is secure is imperative to preventing threats. Tracback's® Mobile Operations Centre saves you the trouble. From the moment we step in, our Mobile Operations Centre never stops scanning your networks, endpoints, and cloud enviroments to eliminate vulnerbilities and misconfigurations. Your Traceback Serviced Security® Team (SST) works with you to implement risk managment process to improve your security posture.


ENHANCED CLOUD MONITORING

Observe. Monitor. Protect. Any threat, any time.

The Cloud can be one of the wildest and most difficult places to secure. Thats why Traceback® is here to help you. Our Mobile Operations Centre detects vulnerabilities and attacks as they occur across multiple SaaS and IaaS platforms. Your Traceback Serviced Security® Team (SST) works directly with you, bringing their cloud security expertise to bear with implentation process, risk surface identification, and ongoing cloud monitoring, enhancing your security posture.
